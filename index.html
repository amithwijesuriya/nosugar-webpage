Getting started (your first day)

  1. Create your profile. We give you a starting daily added-sugar budget based on a few basics. You can change it any time.
  2. Log what actually has added sugar. Soda, candy, sauces, cereal, sweet coffee, etc. Just enter grams or search the food and tap add.
  3. Watch the ring. The ring fills toward your daily limit. When it’s full, you’re “at your limit” for the day. That’s your stop signal.
  4. Check Activity. Movement translates into “sugar credit.” Go for a walk, get a few grams back.
  5. Look for patterns after ~4–6 weeks. You’ll start to see where most of your sugar is sneaking in — the repeat habits that matter.

How it works

  1. Set (or accept) your daily limit.
  2. Log a few items. The ring fills toward that limit.
  3. Decide what to cut. That’s literally it.
