nosugar

Cut added sugar with one simple number.

nosugar gives you a daily added-sugar budget, shows how fast you're burning through it, and helps you stop when you're “done for the day.” No calorie math. No nutrition overwhelm.

Getting started

  1. Create your profile. We give you a starting daily added-sugar budget based on a few basics. You can change it any time.
  2. Log what actually has added sugar. Soda, candy, sauces, cereal, sweet coffee, etc. Just type the grams/oz of added sugar or search the food and tap add.
  3. Watch the ring. The ring fills toward your daily limit. When it’s full, you’re “at your limit” for the day. That’s your stop signal.
  4. Check Activity. Movement translates into “sugar credit.” Go for a walk, get a few grams back.
  5. Look for patterns after ~4–6 weeks. You’ll start to see where most of your sugar is sneaking in — the repeat habits that matter.
  6. Review your data. Every week, nosugar shows your total added sugar and highlights your highest and lowest days so you can spot problem spikes.
  7. Export your data. Export your entries weekly or monthly to see long-term sugar habits and how they’re trending.
