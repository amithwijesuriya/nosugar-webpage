Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2025-10-21

Welcome to nosugar. We respect your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and the choices you have.

Summary: We collect account details (email and optional name), optional profile info (height, weight, units), and app logs you enter (added-sugar entries and activities). We use Supabase to store and process this data securely. We don’t sell your data.

Information We Collect

  • Account Data: Email address and password hash (via Supabase Auth); optional display name.
  • Profile Data (optional): Height, weight, sex, unit preferences.
  • Log Data: Entries you add (e.g., items and grams of added sugar), activity entries.
  • Device/Technical: IP address, device/browser info, and minimal analytics for reliability/performance.

How We Use Data

  • Provide and personalize the app (daily limit estimates, progress visuals).
  • Sync across your signed-in devices (Supabase).
  • Improve features, troubleshoot, and protect against abuse.
  • Send important updates and security notices.

Legal Bases (EEA/UK)

Performance of contract (provide the service), consent (optional features), and legitimate interests (security and reliability). You can withdraw consent any time in-app or by contacting us.

Sharing

We don’t sell personal information. We share data with service providers (e.g., Supabase) under data-processing agreements, or when required by law.

Retention

We keep data while your account is active. You can delete your account in Settings → Data & Export → Delete account. Minimal records may be retained as required by law.

Your Rights

  • Access, correction, portability, and deletion.
  • Object or restrict certain processing.
  • California (CCPA): right to know/delete and opt-out of sale/share (we do not sell).

Contact: nosugarhelp@gmail.com

Children

nosugar isn’t intended for children under 13 (or higher local minimum). We don’t knowingly collect children’s data.

Security

We use industry-standard measures and Supabase security. No method is 100% secure.

Changes

We may update this policy. We’ll post updates here with a new “Last updated” date.
