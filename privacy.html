Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2025-10-21

Welcome to nosugar. We respect your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and the choices you have.

Information We Collect

  • Account Data: Email and password hash (via Supabase Auth); optional display name.
  • Profile Data (optional): Height, weight, sex, unit preferences.
  • Log Data: Entries you add (grams of added sugar), activity entries.
  • Technical: IP, device/browser info, and minimal analytics for reliability/performance.

How We Use Data

  • Provide and personalize the app (daily limit estimates, progress visuals).
  • Sync across devices (Supabase).
  • Improve features, troubleshoot, and protect against abuse.
  • Send important updates and security notices.

Sharing

We don’t sell personal data. We share with service providers (e.g., Supabase) under data-processing agreements, or when required by law.

Retention

We keep data while your account is active. You can delete your account in Settings → Account → Delete account. Minimal records may be retained as required by law.

Your Rights

  • Access, correction, portability, and deletion.
  • Object or restrict processing in some cases.
