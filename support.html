FAQ

How do I reset my password?

Use “Forgot password” on the sign-in screen. Check spam if you don’t see the email.

How do I delete my account?

Open the app → Settings → Data & Export → Delete account, or email us from your account email.

How can I export my data?

Open the app → Settings → Data & Export → Export to download a CSV.
