Data & Deletion

Last updated: 2025-10-21

This page explains how to export and delete your data.

Export

  1. Open the app → Settings → Data & Export.
  2. Select Export CSV to download your logs.

Delete Account & Data

  1. Open the app → Settings → Data & Export → Delete account.
  2. Confirm deletion. This permanently removes your account and associated data from our systems (subject to short backups/retention for security/compliance).

You can also email nosugarhelp@gmail.com from your account email to request deletion.

Timing

Deletion is initiated immediately and typically completes within 30 days. You’ll lose access once deletion is processed.
